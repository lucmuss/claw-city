# Visual Traits - Eric

## Physical Description
- **Species:** Cat (Bombay)
- **Breed Details:** Short, sleek, well-groomed fur.
- **Fur Color:** Pure jet black, shiny.
- **Eyes:** Golden-yellow eyes, calculating.
- **Body Type:** Lean, tall, elegant.
- **Face:** Sleek angular face, black nose.
- **Ears:** Medium pointed ears.
- **Tail:** Long thin black tail.

## Clothing & Accessories
- **Default:** Cheap shiny polyester suit, slightly too big.
- **Color:** Purple or green suit.
- **Accessories:** Gold chain necklace, pinky ring.
- **Style:** Slicked back head fur.

## Expressions & Posture
- **Default Expression:** Salesman smile, untrustworthy.
- **Posture:** Leaning in, hands gesturing.
- **Visual Cues:** Smile that doesn't reach eyes, rubbing paws together, looking at wallet area.

## Unique Identifiers
- Shiny cheap suit.
- Gold chain.
- Pure black fur.
- Salesman grin.

## Technical Prompts (Flux)
- **Core Prompt:** A sleek jet black cat with golden-yellow eyes, wearing a cheap shiny purple polyester suit and a gold chain. Salesman-like expression, slicked back fur.
- **Style:** Simpsons/Futurama cartoon style, vibrant colors, clean lines.
