# Visual Traits - Tina

## Physical Description
- **Species:** Cat (Calico)
- **Breed Details:** Short, practical fur.
- **Fur Color:** White, orange, and black patches.
- **Eyes:** Tired but kind hazel eyes.
- **Body Type:** Average, hardworking build.
- **Face:** Friendly approachable face, half pink half black nose.
- **Ears:** Medium ears, one orange and one black.
- **Tail:** Multicolored calico tail.

## Clothing & Accessories
- **Default:** Diner waitress uniform.
- **Colors:** Pink uniform dress, white apron.
- **Accessories:** Notepad in apron, pen behind ear.
- **Footwear:** Comfortable worn shoes.

## Expressions & Posture
- **Default Expression:** Tired but friendly, seen-it-all look.
- **Posture:** Slightly slouched, always carrying something.
- **Visual Cues:** Blowing fur out of face, balancing multiple plates, knowing eye-roll.

## Unique Identifiers
- Calico three-color pattern.
- Pink diner uniform.
- Notepad and pen.
- Tired friendly face.

## Technical Prompts (Flux)
- **Core Prompt:** A tired but friendly three-colored calico cat (white, orange, black) wearing a pink diner waitress uniform and a white apron. Hazel eyes, balancing plates, pen behind her ear.
- **Style:** Simpsons/Futurama cartoon style, vibrant colors, clean lines.
